### Microsoft 365 Copilot の会議・チャット機能を更新

- 原題: Microsoft Teams: Delete meeting generated content in recap
- ソース: Microsoft 365 Roadmap
- 公開日: 2026/03/17
- リリース段階: In development
- 役割タグ: 管理者向け / 作成者向け
- URL: https://www.microsoft.com/microsoft-365/roadmap?id=557170

Meeting organizers can now easily delete meeting generated content including recording, transcript, AI summary, and notes through the new delete button in recap page. Shared files in the meeting won't be deleted.
