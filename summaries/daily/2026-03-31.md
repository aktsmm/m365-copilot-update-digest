### Microsoft 365 Copilot の会議・チャット機能を更新

- 原題: Outlook: Copilot provides coaching feedback in chat as you draft, edit and format emails
- ソース: Microsoft 365 Roadmap
- 公開日: 2026/03/31
- リリース段階: In development
- 役割タグ: 管理者向け / 作成者向け
- URL: https://www.microsoft.com/microsoft-365/roadmap?id=559418

Copilot now provides coaching feedback on email drafts via chat and can apply suggestions directly to your message in Outlook. Users have the option to choose which suggestions are applied, and Copilot updates your email in place.
